Welcome to the Intervention

Back to Intervention Access

Welcome! This intervention is a research-based online program to promote health-enhancing physical activity in adults who are overweight or obese. For simplicity, “adults who are overweight or obese” will be referred to as “adults with obesity” from this point forward.

This intervention is based on scientific evidence, behavioral theory, and effective behavioral change techniques to help adults with obesity: (a) learn major health benefits of physical activity, (b) motivate for physical activity, (c) understand how to be physically active, and (d) overcome their unique barriers to physical activity. The references used to develop the intervention can be provided upon request. The intervention is developed and tested by an interdisciplinary research team that includes experts in health science, computer science, art and design, theatre, etc.

We believe that you can promote physical activity with this intervention! To get you started, let's learn more about the intervention contents.
